Why to use AI?

Precision - AI and ML technology use algorithms to analyze data and make predictions based on that information. AI programs can be at least 95% accurate on a regular basis
Work Non-Stop - One of the most basic difference between AI and Human is AI can never become tired and so it can work non-stop
Predict from data - AI has been used in predicting future. For example, predicting climate, moving object, Stockmarket, recruitment, banking, etc. AI is able to predict the future state of a system based on past data.
Complex data - There are tons of data generated every single day. AI is capable of analysing the data and make useful decisions and in a way those decision taking can help to ease human life.
