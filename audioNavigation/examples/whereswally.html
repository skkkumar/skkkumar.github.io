Find the cats

Best experienced over headphones.

The aim of this game is to find 3 cats. Press left, right, up, down arrow keys to move your character. You are only able to hear the cats. When you are close by, you have found the cat. Only one cat will come out of its hiding at a time. We will record the time it takes you to finish the game. Try to improve your timing. Press space bar to start the game.
