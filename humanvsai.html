Babies have eyes, nose, ears, skin to sense the world around them.
AI have Camera sensors, Microphones, tactile sensors, etc to sense the world around them.
Babies acquire knowledge from parents, and the environment and learn from it.
AI acquires data from the sensors and learns from it.
Babies learn from repeated experiences like associating names with parents' faces.
AI learns from multiple instances of training samples where face is input and name is output.
Babies have limited knowledge of the world and so they make mistakes.
AI initially might have limited training data, due to which it might not predict accurately always.
Babies communicate using speech, gestures, action, facial expressions, etc.
AI can communicate its prediction through speech, gestures (In robots), audio, visual, text, graphs, etc.
